Sarah's Favorite Movies


Little Miss Sunshine

Little Miss Sunshine is a sweet movie about a little girl who wants to become a part of a beauty pageant. This movie is filled with comedy and warmness.





Aladin

Aladin was my favorite movie since the first time I saw it. It made me see "a whole new world." "When wishing upon a star," I wish I had Iago as a sidekick. This movie is wonderful and forever my favorite Disney film.
